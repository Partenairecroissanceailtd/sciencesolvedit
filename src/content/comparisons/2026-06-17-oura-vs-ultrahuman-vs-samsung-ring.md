## Oura Ring 5 🏆

**Best for:** People who want the most accurate, most validated sleep and recovery tracking.

**Pros:** Best-in-class accuracy, AI Advisor, largest features set, proven hardware
**Cons:** $5.99/month subscription needed, no workout auto-detection

**Verdict:** The gold standard. If you want the best data and don't mind a subscription, this is the choice.

---

## Amazfit Helio Ring

**Best for:** People who want quality tracking without monthly fees.

**Pros:** No subscription, fast 1.4-hour charging, titanium build, Zepp ecosystem
**Cons:** Newer product, less validation data, smaller user community

**Verdict:** Excellent value. Similar feature set to Oura at a lower price with no ongoing costs.

---

## RingConn Gen 2 Air

**Best for:** Battery life champions.

**Pros:** 10-day battery, ultra-thin (2mm), no subscription
**Cons:** Newer company, smaller app, fewer features

**Verdict:** The best choice if you hate charging devices. Battery life is a genuine differentiator.
