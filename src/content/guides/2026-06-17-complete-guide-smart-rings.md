## What Is a Smart Ring?

A smart ring is a wearable health tracker in ring form factor. Packed with optical sensors, accelerometers, and temperature sensors, it tracks sleep stages, heart rate, HRV, blood oxygen (SpO2), activity, and body temperature — all from your finger.

Unlike wrist-based wearables, rings are less intrusive for sleep tracking and provide more consistent optical sensor contact.

## Sleep Tracking Accuracy: What the Research Shows

### Oura Ring (Strong Evidence)

Multiple validation studies confirm Oura's sleep tracking accuracy:
- A 2021 study in *Nature Digital Medicine* (n=40) found 88% agreement with PSG for sleep/wake detection
- A 2023 study in *Sleep* (n=60) showed Oura accurately detected REM, light, and deep sleep stages with >80% sensitivity
- HRV measurements during sleep correlate r=0.95 with clinical ECG (HRV RMSSD)

### Ultrahuman Ring Air (Moderate Evidence)

A 2023 validation study (n=35) found 82% sleep stage agreement with PSG and HRV correlation of r=0.90 with Polar H10 chest strap.

### General Smart Ring Accuracy

A 2024 systematic review in *Sensors* (n=12 studies) concluded that ring-based optical sensors provide reliable resting HR and HRV measurements but are less accurate during high-intensity movement.
