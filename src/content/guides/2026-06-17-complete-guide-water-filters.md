## The Science of Water Filtration

### Contaminant Reduction (Strong Evidence)

Multiple studies confirm the effectiveness of home water filtration:

- **Carbon filtration** (Brita, PUR): Reduces chlorine taste/odor, lead (99%), mercury, copper, and some pesticides. Does NOT remove fluoride, arsenic, nitrate, or most dissolved solids.
- **Ion exchange** (ZeroWater): Removes 99% of total dissolved solids — the only pitcher filter to achieve near-zero TDS.
- **Reverse osmosis** (under-sink): Removes 90-99% of all contaminants, including fluoride, arsenic, nitrates, and PFAS.

A 2021 study in *Environmental Science & Technology* found that even basic carbon filters significantly reduced microplastic particles in tap water (from 5 particles/L to <1 particle/L).

### PFAS Removal

A 2023 study from the *Journal of Environmental Engineering* tested 10 home water filters for PFAS (forever chemicals) removal:
- **Activated carbon filters:** 50-85% PFAS removal (varies by brand)
- **Ion exchange (ZeroWater):** 95-99% PFAS removal
- **Reverse osmosis:** 99%+ PFAS removal
