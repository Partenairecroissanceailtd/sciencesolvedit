Getting the most out of your infrared sauna blanket requires more than just lying down and turning it on. Based on the research, here's the optimal protocol.

---

## Before Your Session

### Hydrate
The single most important step. Drink 16-24oz of water 30 minutes before your session. Consider adding electrolytes for sessions over 30 minutes.

### Timing
- **Morning:** Best for alertness and metabolic boost
- **Evening:** Best for relaxation — the post-sauna temperature drop promotes sleep onset
- **Post-workout:** Best for muscle recovery

### What to Wear
The included towel insert (organic cotton recommended) worn inside the blanket. This absorbs sweat and protects the blanket liner. Nothing else needed.

---

## During Your Session

### Optimal Settings

| Experience Level | Temperature | Duration | Frequency |
|----------------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Beginner | 120-140°F | 15-20 min | 2-3x/week |
| Intermediate | 140-160°F | 25-35 min | 3-4x/week |
| Advanced | 160-176°F | 35-45 min | 4-5x/week |

### Heat Zones (if your blanket has them)
- **Full body:** Standard setting, all zones on
- **Targeted recovery:** Zone-specific heat for lower body, back, or shoulders
- **Cool-down:** Reduce to lowest setting for last 5 minutes

### What to Do During
- **Read** (a book, not a phone — EMF concern with close proximity)
- **Meditate or breathe** — deep breathing amplifies the parasympathetic response
- **Listen to audio** (podcast, music, audiobook)
- **Do NOT** use your phone in direct contact with the blanket

## After Your Session

### Cool Down
1. Unzip and sit up slowly
2. Stand and stretch for 2-3 minutes
3. Take a lukewarm shower (not ice cold — rapid cooling can shock the system)
4. Hydrate with another 16-24oz of water

### Clean Your Blanket
- Wipe down the interior with a damp cloth
- Let it air dry completely before storing
- Wash the towel insert regularly

### Rest
The benefits continue for 30-60 minutes post-session as your body cools down. Avoid intense activity immediately after.

## Protocols for Specific Goals

### Recovery Protocol (Post-Workout)
1. Cool down from exercise (5 min light stretching)
2. Hydrate (16oz)
3. Sauna blanket at 160°F for 20-25 minutes
4. Cold shower (30 sec) → warm shower (2 min) — contrast therapy
5. Protein-rich meal within 60 minutes

### Sleep Protocol (Evening Use)
1. Finish session 60-90 minutes before bed
2. Sauna blanket at 140°F for 25-30 minutes
3. Lukewarm shower
4. Sleep in a cool room (65-68°F)
5. The post-sauna core temperature drop mimics natural sleep onset

### Cardiovascular Protocol
1. 3-4 sessions per week minimum (research shows 4+ sessions/week for cardiovascular benefit)
2. 30-40 minutes at 160-176°F
3. Consistent schedule — irregular use doesn't produce the same vascular adaptations
