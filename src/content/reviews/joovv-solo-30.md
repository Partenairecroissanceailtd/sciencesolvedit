## The Science

Red light therapy works via cytochrome c oxidase absorption in mitochondria, stimulating ATP production. Multiple meta-analyses confirm benefits for:

- **Skin health** — collagen production, wound healing (Avci et al., 2013)
- **Muscle recovery** — reduced DOMS, accelerated repair (Ferraresi et al., 2016)
- **Joint pain** — osteoarthritis symptom reduction (Brosseau et al., 2019)

Joovv uses the clinically validated 660nm (red) and 850nm (near-infrared) wavelengths at 100 mW/cm² — within the therapeutic window of 5-200 mW/cm² identified in the literature.
